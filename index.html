Profile

Surgeon and educator.

I'm an orthopaedic surgeon and a Professor in the College of Medicine at National Chung Hsing University — two roles I hold in equal measure. At Changhua Christian Hospital, I serve as Deputy Director of the Department of Orthopedics and Chief of Pediatric Orthopaedics, caring for patients across pediatric orthopaedics, trauma, and joint reconstruction, with a particular focus on hip-preservation surgery and developmental dysplasia of the hip (DDH).

In the classroom, I teach and mentor the next generation of physicians and serve as an OSCE examiner for Taiwan's medical-education board. Underpinning both is a Ph.D. in biomedical imaging and a commitment to translational research — reflected in multiple patented orthopaedic devices, several of them for neonatal hip care.

To me, operating and teaching are two expressions of the same purpose: better care for patients today, and better-prepared physicians for the patients of tomorrow.
